## About (2,000 chars max)

Pathos Labs works with founders, CEOs, and senior executives on the gap between what they know and what others receive.

The problem is never ability. Not once, in any of the work we've done, has the person across from us lacked the capacity to do the thing. What's missing is the capacity to make that capability visible — in real time, under pressure, to the people who need to see it.

We diagnose where the gap opens, build new defaults through live rehearsal against real stakes, and stay in it until the change holds under pressure without prompting. That's the work. It's precise, it's fast, and the relationship matters — because this kind of change doesn't happen without trust.

Clients include Morgan Stanley, Church of England, YuLife, Relation Therapeutics, and KYC360.
